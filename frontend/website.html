About

MediLens AI combines text retrieval, image retrieval and large language models to help users quickly understand healthcare documents while preserving document context.

Medical reports are dense, inconsistent, and often written for clinicians rather than the people who actually receive them. A single discharge summary can span pages of lab values, imaging notes and dosage instructions — MediLens AI reads that document once, indexes it properly, and lets you ask it questions the way you would ask a person, instead of scrolling and searching by hand.

The problem

Patients and caregivers routinely leave appointments with reports they don't fully understand. Important details get buried in medical terminology, scanned images, and tables that don't translate well to plain language.

Our approach

We split every upload into searchable text and image chunks, embed them, and retrieve only the pieces relevant to your question before Gemini answers — so responses stay grounded in your actual document, not a guess.

How it works

  1. 01

    Upload

    Add one or more PDF reports — labs, prescriptions, discharge notes, imaging.

  2. 02

    Index

    Text is chunked with Sentence Transformers and stored in FAISS; images are indexed with CLIP.

  3. 03

    Ask

    Ask a question in plain English and get an answer grounded in the retrieved passages.

  4. 04

    Review

    See the supporting images alongside the answer so you can verify it against the source.
